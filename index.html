COBB COUNTY’S BEST PRIVATE SCHOOL

The Walker School has again been named the Best College Prep Private School, Best Private High School and Best Private K-12 school in Cobb County by Niche.

Read more

Walker's overall grade in Niche's Best Schools & Districts rankings was an A+. Niche grades and rankings are calculated using dozens of public data sets and millions of reviews. Schools are graded on Academics, Clubs & Activities, College Prep, Diversity, Sports and Teachers. Walker received no lower than an A-minus in each of the categories.

Some of the data points used to calculate a school's overall grade include SAT/ACT scores, top colleges score (average score of colleges that students are most interested in or go on to attend, based on Niche Best Colleges ranking), college enrollment (percent of students who go on to four-year colleges), culture & diversity, parent/student surveys on the overall experience and student-teacher ratio.

THE WALKER HONOR CODE

Through Honor Codes in each division, Walker provides an environment in which our community learns to value honesty and respect others. All students and faculty are guided by the belief that honor is a way of life rather than a set of rules to follow, and the expectation is that our graduates will carry these values with them after they leave our campus.

Read more

The Walker Honor Code’s four principles are: truth, respect of property, academic honesty and defending the Honor Code. These principles require students to be truthful in their relationships with each other and faculty – and respectful of each other’s property – for the express purpose of making the school a better place.

A Well-rounded education

DISCOVER YOUR PASSIONS

Walker students are well-rounded and have the ability to dive into specific areas of academic and extracurricular interests while building skills across the board. We believe that students should have a mastery of a broad range of subjects with a focus on learning over competition, as well as the chance to explore multiple opportunities and try new activities.

Read more

At Walker, you can star in the Fall Musical, represent your school on the soccer field and design your own genetic research project.

ATHLETICS

Walker is committed to fielding strong teams in all sports and our aim is for our students to compete with great enthusiasm and to demonstrate exemplary sportsmanship. 72 percent of students in grades 6-12 play a sport and 30 percent of students play more than one sport.

Walker has over 50 total teams and prioritizes physical education, movement and fun for the Primary and Lower School students.

ARTS

At Walker, students are given the freedom and the opportunity to explore their imagination. It feeds their souls and fuels their academic success.

As early as the Primary School, our students find themselves on-stage in dramatic performances and seated in a studio creating thoughtful works of art. Walker educators are practicing artists themselves— painters, sculptors, musicians, writers and directors. With these connections to the art community, students who pursue the arts have the chance to participate in gallery exhibitions outside of Walker, in musical and dramatic festivals and competitions. 

Learn More

ACADEMICS

We prepare students for college by creating critical thinkers, speakers and writers who are ready to thrive in a changing world. 

Walker’s unique college preparatory curriculum and high level of academic expectation, combined with the support of its accomplished faculty and their close relationships with students, provide students with the freedom to personalize their academic experience and meet their individual goals.
