WALKER SCHOOL

ADMISSION PROCESS

Discover the step-by-step guide to the Walker admission process.

We look forward to getting to know you and your child through this journey. Please create an account in Ravenna. You will find The Walker School listed in the directory of schools in the Atlanta area. After selecting Walker, you will have the option to “explore”, register for an event, or apply for admission. You will see our checklist of application steps and learn more about the application process. We appreciate your interest in The Walker School, and we look forward to getting to know you and your child through this journey.

New Avenues applicants: Visit our New Avenues Dyslexia Program page.

Below are the steps to apply:

STep 1: Connect With Walker

Relationships are key to the Walker experience, and ours begins when you reach out to us. Please complete our inquiry form and create an account in our on-line application management portal, Ravenna. Once you have an account, you will find information about our application process, including invitations to open houses, small group tours and shadow visits (grades 2-12).

Inquire  Apply
STep 2: learn More About Walker

Experience Walker firsthand! We offer numerous opportunities for prospective families to get to know our school community.

PLAN YOUR VISIT
STep 3: Complete Application and Submit Forms

Complete the online application in your Ravenna account and submit the $85 non-refundable application fee. Here are step-by-step instructions for using Ravenna: Ravenna Family Guide.

Within your Ravenna account, you are able to request the teacher and principal/guidance counselor evaluations. All records must come directly from the applicant’s school.

For Primary and Lower School applicants:
We ask for an evaluation from a current teacher. If the applicant has not attended school yet or has been home-schooled, the teacher evaluation will be waived. For Lower School applicants, ask the applicant’s school for an official and current transcript to be uploaded to Ravenna or sent directly to the Walker admission team at admissionforms@thewalkerschool.org. For Kindergarten through Grade 5, the JATP is optional.

For Middle and Upper School applicants:
We request evaluations from the current English teacher, math teacher and principal/guidance counselor. Ask the applicant’s school for an official and current transcript to be uploaded to Ravenna or sent directly to the Walker admission team at admissionforms@thewalkerschool.org. Transcripts must include standardized testing. If no standardized testing is available, please sign up for admission testing with the SSAT website (Secondary School Admission Test). Test results must be sent directly to Walker from SSAT.

Our SSAT school code is 8008.
Step 4: Schedule an observation, shadow visit and/or interview

For Primary and Lower School applicants: Once you have applied, register your child for the required student observation/assessment visit through your Ravenna account. On-campus observation/assessment visits are scheduled to begin in late December, early January.

For Grades 2-5: In addition to the required student assessment, we offer optional shadow visits to give applicants the opportunity to spend the day meeting our students and faculty and visiting classes. Once you have applied, register your child for the Lower School Assessment & Shadow Visit through your Ravenna account.

For Middle and Upper School applicants: An in-person or virtual student interview is required as part of the application process. We also offer optional shadow visits to give applicants the opportunity to spend the day meeting our students and faculty and visiting classes. Register your child for a shadow visit through your Ravenna account. If you have applied, your child's interview will take place during the shadow visit.
STep 5: Apply for Financial Assistance (optional)

We are proud to support 31% of our students through our financial aid budget and are committed to working very hard with every family to try to make a Walker education possible. All awards are based on demonstrated need and the availability of funds in our current-year budget; awards at Walker are not based on merit.

Financial Assistance Application and Supporting Documents Deadlines:

  • October 1, 2026: Applications Open
  • November 30, 2026: Current Families
  • February 5, 2027: New Families

Full instructions and detailed information on applying for financial assistance can be found on the Tuition & Financial Assistance page.
Step 6: Track Your Application

Track your application progress using your Ravenna account. You will be able to see when each step is due and when each one is complete. Admission decisions for Early Learner, PreK, and New Avenues applications are communicated on a rolling basis.

Important Dates:

  • October 1, 2026: Applications Open
  • February 5, 2027: Application Deadline
  • February 26, 2027: Supporting Documents Deadline
  • April 3, 2027: Enrollment Decisions Communicated

Financial Assistance Application and Supporting Documents Deadlines:

  • October 1, 2026: Applications Open
  • November 30, 2026: Application Deadline for Returning Families
  • February 5, 2027: Application Deadline for New Families

For on-time Kindergarten through Grade 12 applications, admission decisions will be posted in Ravenna on April 3, 2027, at 8 a.m.
