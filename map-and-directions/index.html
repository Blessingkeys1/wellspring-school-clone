ABOUT US

MAPS AND DIRECTIONS

Our Main Campus is located at 700 Cobb Pkwy N.

Our Primary School Campus is located at 830 Damar Road behind our Main Campus.


DIRECTIONS

  • From I-75 North: Follow I-75 North to Exit 265 for GA-120 Loop. Turn left onto North Marietta Parkway. Turn right onto Cobb Parkway. After the intersection of Cobb Parkway and Allgood Road, Walker will be immediately on your right.
     
  • From I-75 South: Follow I-75 South to Exit 267B for US-41 toward GA-5 S. Turn left onto Cobb Parkway, and Walker will be down the hill on your left. 
     
  • From Historic Marietta Square: Take North Marietta Parkway / 120 Loop. Turn left onto Fairground Street. Take the first exit through the traffic circle onto Allgood Road and use the second from the left turn lane to turn left onto Cobb Parkway. Walker will be immediately on your right.

 

EXPLORE THE WALKER SCHOOL CAMPUS

Click on the numbers below to find out more about our school's outstanding facilities:

Gatti Hall

Gatti Hall is the heart of the Lower School, serving as a versatile gathering space for students and teachers. Transformed from a lunch room to an assembly hall, indoor playground and much more, the space allows students from all grade levels to build community, foster new friendships and promote a culture of inclusivity.

Lower School

The Lower School building, filled with vibrant classrooms and students excited for learning, is a hub of creativity and discovery for our young Wolverines. From the cheerful hum of learning rotations to the cozy library, every corner of the Lower School building is designed to inspire curiosity and build a lifelong love of learning.

Coles Athletic Center

In Coles Athletic Center, student-athletes can hone their skills, achieve their athletic goals, and work to become the next generation of athletic excellence. The gym is furnished with practice and competition courts for volleyball and basketball, a designated wrestling room, a fully furnished weight room and an indoor track. 

Coca-Cola Family Auditorium

Originally the school’s gymnasium, the Coca-Cola Auditorium was transformed into a haven for student performers to cultivate and grow their skills. The Coca-Cola Family Auditorium hosts many events throughout the school year including concerts, annual musicals, assemblies, guest speakers and much more. 

Patty H. Mozley Studio Theatre

The Patty H. Mozley Studio Theatre honors Patty Mozley, the former teacher then Director of Admissions who devoted 43 years to The Walker School. The black-box-style theatre is home to annual play performances, rehearsals, and drama classes, cultivating confidence and creativity within our school community. 

Admission

The Admission Office is where all students and families looking to plant new roots at Walker get started. The admission team offers tours, information sessions, and much more to allow new families to get to know the culture and community at Walker. 

Administrative Offices

Administrative Offices are located at the heart of campus just past the front lobby.

Hubert Middle School

The Hubert Middle School, located at the center of campus, is a nurturing environment where students navigate their academic and personal growth. Filled with classrooms and fueled by curiosity, the Middle School encourages academic excellence with a robust curriculum and wide offerings of extracurriculars. 

Upper School

Allowing students to pursue academic excellence while continuing to foster their curiosity, the Upper School classrooms are a host to concentration programs in Public Health, Engineering, and Humanities. The Upper School offers choice electives in fine arts, English and STEM fields, and connects students with college counselors.

Bowden Plaza 

The Bowden Plaza is an outdoor gathering space designed for Middle and Upper School students to utilize during their lunch, recess and break periods. The picnic tables create a popular outdoor lunch space and study area. 

Bowden Library

Bowden Library is more than books, it is home to many classrooms, computer labs, and study areas. Students seeking a quiet study space, enriching literature, detailed research or creative inspiration can find all they need in Bowden Library. 

Dining Hall

The Dining Hall serves all Middle and Upper School students, faculty and staff. Dining options provided by Flik Food Service include a hot meal, salad bar, sandwich station, panini grill and much more. 

Muthiah Activity Center

Muthiah Activity Center provides space for students of all ages to participate in athletic programs. With designated spaces for basketball, football, cheer, volleyball, and much more, the recently renovated Muthiah Activity Center inspires growth for future champions. 

Warren Science & Technology Building

The Warren Science & Technology Building was designed to encourage student curiosity and exploration in the STEM field. Equipped with 15 classrooms and labs, two research studios, a recording studio and a sound booth, there are opportunities for students to pursue academic excellence and further explore their interests. 

Parking

Students who choose to drive to campus are given a special parking area to allow a safe and efficient flow of traffic, divided by grade level. 

Tennis Courts 

The Walker School has six tennis courts across two different locations on campus. The two court areas allow matches and practices to happen simultaneously on campus. 

Tennis Courts 

The Walker School has six tennis courts across two different locations on campus. The two court areas allow matches and practices to happen simultaneously on campus. 

Robertson Field

Robertson Field is a practice and competition area for the football, lacrosse and soccer teams. Built to promote athletic development among students, the newly renovated turf field allows students to practice and prepare for their sport during any season. The fan stands provide an excellent view of the action in an open-air viewing area. 

Practice Field

The Practice Field was designed to allow multiple teams to compete and practice simultaneously on campus. With over 45 teams across the middle and upper schools, the practice field plays a vital role in the success of the Walker Athletics Program. 

Batting Facility

The Indoor Batting Facility is located on the lower practice field, allowing student-athletes to practice in any weather condition. 

Primary School

The Primary School is where the adventure of learning begins at The Walker School. Vibrant classrooms, a specialized library and an art room allow children to lay the foundation for a lifetime of learning. 

Warren Baseball Field

The Warren Baseball Field serves as a practice and playing facility for the Upper School baseball teams. Here, students sharpen their skills, build friendships and aspire to become future champions.

Middle School Baseball Field

The Middle School Baseball Field, located at 815 Allgood Rd. is an open-air facility for rising student-athletes to hone their skills before graduating to the larger facility. Students work hard both in the classroom and on the field to prepare for Upper School. 

 

Softball Field

The Softball Field, located at 815 Allgood Rd. is an open-air facility for all student softball players to practice and play. Friendships and teamwork transcend the field and continue their foundation in the classroom.
