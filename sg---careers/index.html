CAREERS AT THE WALKER

Welcome, and thank you for your interest in a position at The Walker School. We are metro Atlanta’s top college-preparatory independent school for students in PK3 through 12th grade seeking an engaging educational experience within an intimately scaled, caring and diverse community.

For all positions, we look for candidates who are passionate about working with young people, value lifelong learning, will fully engage in the life of the school and are committed to fostering an inclusive and equitable community.
