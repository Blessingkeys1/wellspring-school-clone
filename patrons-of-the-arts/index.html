COMMUNITY

PATRONS OF THE ARTS

Patrons of the Arts (POTA) is an organization of parents who support all of the expressions of the fine arts at The Walker School by supporting the teachers & creative students.

Become a POTA Partner if you are interested in directly supporting the arts.

Email 
 


 

POTA stands for “Patrons of The Arts” but it could just as easily represent “Parents of The Arts.” Walker parents created POTA in 2008 to support all fine arts at The Walker School. We support each fine arts department from Primary to Upper School with volunteers and by providing items for the classrooms that benefit all students whether or not they participate in a fine arts extracurricular activity.

EXPERIENCE
POTA

POTA’s Impact on the Arts

We collaborate with Walker’s fine arts faculty to identify and allocate funding to supplies, equipment, education opportunities, field trips, scholarships, and much more. 

Attend a Planning Meeting 

POTA meets monthly on the third Thursday at 8 a.m. in the Boardroom next to the Dining Hall. 

Volunteer 

There are many opportunities to volunteer with Walker Arts. 

Support POTA 

A portion of all Twin Engine Coffee purchases will be donated to POTA. Click here for more information and a printable message to include with your gift.
