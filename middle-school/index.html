MIDDLE SCHOOL

#onewalker

LIFE IN THE MIDDLE CAN BE MAGICAL!

Students explore who they are and begin to develop good habits and strong character.

Walker’s Middle School program is designed to provide students with the freedom to discover their interests while preparing them with the study and organization skills they need to succeed.

EVERY STUDENT IS KNOWN

Walker is built on the strength of its community. We want our student body to reflect the world around us with individual strengths that are encouraged and celebrated.

WHY WE ARE UNIQUE

The Walker Middle School is fun! This is a time of extraordinary academic and social-emotional growth. Students are encouraged to try new things and participate in a variety of athletics and after-school activities in the arts and technology.

SUPPORT AT WALKER

RELATIONSHIPS ARE OUR FOUNDATION

Students have multiple layers of support and are well-known by many trusted adults, including their teachers, small-group advisors, grade-level deans, learning specialists, coaches and administrators.

Students are held to high standards and enjoy small class sizes, allowing them to thrive in a learning environment that is positive and nurturing.

A rounded education

DISCOVER YOUR PASSIONS

Click on the sections to the right to learn more about athletics, arts and academics at Walker.

ATHLETICS

More than 70 percent of our Middle School students participate in athletics.

The Middle School has 26 teams spanning 14 different sports. At the Middle School level, the focus of athletics is on fundamentals and growth through practice and competition. This is a time to help athletes develop a love for the game and the necessary skills required to compete at the varsity level.

Learn more

ARTS

The Middle School Arts program gives students the opportunity to practice their creativity and explore the techniques and skills of each discipline.

Students have performance opportunities and showcase their artwork in galleries throughout the campus. Band, orchestra and chorus students perform several concerts throughout the year. Middle School Drama students perform in both large-scale musicals in our auditorium and intimately staged plays in our studio theater multiple times each year.

Learn more

ACADEMICS

Middle School students are given space to try new things, make mistakes, solve problems, feel supported and be celebrated.

With an average class size of 16 students, every child is known, and every voice is valued. Dynamic English and writing instruction, dedicated science labs, three world languages and five levels of math instruction are just the beginning. Fine arts, technology and physical education round out a busy day in the life of a middle-schooler.
