CAREERS AT WALKER

Thank you for your interest in working at The Walker School. We are metro Atlanta’s top college-preparatory independent school for students in PK3 through 12th grade seeking an engaging educational experience within an intimately scaled, caring and diverse community.

For all positions, we look for candidates who are passionate about working with young people, value lifelong learning, will fully engage in the life of the school and are committed to fostering an inclusive and equitable community.

WORKING AT WALKER

The Walker School is a leader among independent schools in the metro Atlanta area and has long been recognized for its first-rate academic program, close relationships between students and faculty, and abundant opportunities for students to engage in meaningful experiences both in and out of the classroom. Walker enrolls students from Pre-K3 through Grade 12.

Our commitment to diversity provides a welcoming and safe atmosphere for students to discover who they are and to successfully navigate an ever-changing world. The Walker School is proud to provide equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants without regard to race, color, sex, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, age, or any other category protected by federal, state, or local law.

BENEFITS

The Walker School strives to provide the best possible work environment and benefits for all employees. Walker's insurance, disability and retirement benefits are varied. The school reserves the right to alter benefits as deemed necessary by the Board of Trustees.

Employment Benefit Summary Sheet
EQUAL OPPORTUNITY EMPLOYER

The Walker School is proud to provide equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants without regard to race, color, sex, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, age or any other category protected by federal, state or local law. Cultural competence is an expectation of employment for all professional staff. We believe in the infinite worth of everyone, and this core value is fundamental to our mission as faculty and staff. We are particularly interested in candidates with a proven commitment to teaching and working in a diverse, multicultural setting.
PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Walker faculty members distinguish themselves as lifelong learners and innovators. One of the school’s top priorities is ensuring that our faculty and staff have access to the most relevant and meaningful professional development opportunities. To support that growth, the School offers full-time faculty and staff members funding for:

  • Conferences and workshops
  • Advanced degrees and other coursework
  • Curriculum development

There is also a thriving culture of internal professional development, with teachers regularly visiting one another’s classrooms and outside speakers invited to campus to work with groups of faculty members.
APPLYING for a position

The Walker School is an Equal Opportunity Employer that hires professionals dedicated to serving our school community in their respective capacities. Our employees are experts in their fields who, with the school’s support, seek out opportunities to further develop as professionals. Salaries are commensurate with experience.

Job opportunities are posted here as they become available. If you see a position that fits your qualifications, simply follow the link to apply. All applicants must complete an employment application. The Walker School may also require a résumé, certification, and letters of reference, depending on the position. An employment application must be completed before an applicant is considered a candidate.
