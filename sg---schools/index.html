EVERY CHILD IS KNOWN

With an average class size of 16 students, every child is known, and every voice is valued. Fine arts, technology, and physical education round out a busy day in the life of a middle-schooler. Students have a 30-minute recess break each day to enjoy social time with peers outside.

Advisors and Grade Level Deans work with each student to guide them through these years. Help with self-advocacy, writing skills, public speaking and organization are all part of the Middle School experience. Walker encourages exploration and involvement. It is not uncommon for a student to be involved in football, a middle school drama production and our Aerospace club.
