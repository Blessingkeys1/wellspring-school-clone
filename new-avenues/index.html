NEW AVENUES

#onewalker

HELPING STUDENTS REACH THEIR FULL POTENTIAL

Our innovative New Avenues Dyslexia Program is designed to serve children with dyslexia in grades 1-5 who have above-average academic aptitude and a strong desire to fulfill their abundant potential.

Inquire  Apply

Read More
With dedicated support at school and at home, students in New Avenues work and learn within Walker's college-preparatory environment. The goal is for remediated students to transition into traditional school communities using learned strategies and resources to flourish in the classroom and beyond.

#onewalker

“SUCCESSFUL STUDENTS AND LIFELONG LEARNERS”

“Our New Avenues Dyslexia Program teaches students in ways that best suit their individual learning needs. As students begin to feel success, their self-esteem and self-advocacy skills grow. Our New Avenues students are equipped with the tools and strategies they need to be successful students and lifelong learners.”

- Cindy O’Neill, Head of New Avenues Dyslexia Program

Inquire  Apply

#onewalker

AT WALKER, WE ARE ALL WOLVERINES!

New Avenues students are very much a part of the larger school community and participate with their grade-level peers in co-curriculuar activities including art, science, music, technology, PE, recess and lunch. Our unique “school within a school” model cultivates intellectual curiosity while also providing students with opportunities to develop  a broad social base.

Inquire  Apply

#ONEWALKER

WHY WE ARE UNIQUE

New Avenues students have strong cognitive abilities yet often require additional support and instruction with reading, writing, organization and study skills.

New Avenues teachers are trained in specialized research-based methods, and they are invested in each student’s success, creating a safe environment to take academic risks. Students learn how to become active readers, author their own writing pieces and master math concepts – all while gaining confidence in their voice and ability to learn.
