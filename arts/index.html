EXPAND YOUR CREATIVITY

At Walker, students are given the freedom and the opportunity to explore their imagination. Feeding their souls and fueling their academic success. As early as the Primary School, our students find themselves on-stage in dramatic performances and in a studio creating thoughtful works of art.

Inquire  Apply

Read More
Walker educators are practicing artists themselves—painters, sculptors, musicians, writers and directors. With these connections to the art community, students who pursue the arts in the Upper School have the chance to participate in Advanced Placement courses in visual arts and music and in local gallery exhibitions, musical and dramatic festivals and competitions. Childhood is a time of discovery and there is no better way to learn about yourself and the world around you than through art and artistic expression.

OUR ARTS PROGRAM

The fine arts curriculum includes a variety of courses in theatre, music, and visual art designed to give students the opportunity to practice their creativity and explore the techniques and skills of each discipline. Students have performance opportunities in each division and showcase their artwork in galleries throughout the campus.

Inquire  Apply

Read More
Upper School students can dig deeper into the arts with special electives such as Advanced Dance in Choreography, Stagecraft, Directing, Music Theory, and Public Speaking. Our robust music department includes an award-winning Concert Band, Jazz Band, Pep Band, Pit Orchestra, Orchestra and Show Choir that perform several concerts throughout the year both on and off campus. Our theatre students perform in both large-scale musicals in our auditorium and intimately staged plays in our studio theater multiple times each year.

PERFORMING ARTS

As early as the Primary School, Walker students are encouraged to find their voices both on and off the stage.

This early exposure to being in the spotlight develops students' confidence in speaking and performing in front of large crowds from a young age, while diversified musical and theatrical experiences serve to improve artistic fluency across multiple mediums.

Discover Performing Arts

VISUAL ARTS

Our visual arts faculty inspires and challenges young people both individually and collaboratively as they increase aesthetic awareness, craft a creative spirit, cultivate problem solving, and refine both decision-making skills and a discerning sensibility toward their visual environment.

Visual Arts enriches the learning experience as students learn a common language while working in a variety of media. We embrace the creative process, allowing learners to explore the role of art in their lives and world cultures.

Discover Visual Arts

TECHNICAL ARTS

Our technical arts program offers a plethora of team-building and skill-enhancing opportunities.

Each year Walker students have a chance to perform in musicals and plays across all divisions. With these performances ever-changing, students have opportunities to participate in elaborate set design, stage management, lighting and audio design, and more. Walker’s stagecraft has been nominated for ‘Best Technical Execution.’
