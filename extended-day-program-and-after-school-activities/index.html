THE WALKER SCHOOL

EXTENDED DAY AND AFTER-SCHOOL ACTIVITIES

The Walker Extended Day Program (EDP) is a safe, fun-filled after-school option for current Walker students that provides age-appropriate activities ranging from time for homework and arts and crafts to outdoor physical activity, games and more.

 

Candice McAndrews

Director, Primary and Lower School Extended Day Programs

EMAIL PHONE

 

Walker’s Extended Day Program (EDP) is available from the end of the regular school day (2:30 p.m. for Primary School and 3:15 p.m. for Lower School) until 6:00 p.m. Monday through Friday.

EDP is located in the Multipurpose Room in the Primary School and in Gatti Hall for the Lower School. Walker students can sign up for our unlimited EDP package online during the summer or utilize daily drop-in EDP. 

The Walker School offers our students many engaging and diverse After-School Activities on campus. Sign-up typically begins on WOW Day in August, and additional information will be sent home as the school year begins. 

Some examples of Extended Day Program activities include:

  • Cub Scout Pack 700 
  • Art Workshops
  • Advanced Drawing
  • Knitting Club
  • Intro to Robotics
  • Junior Robotics
  • Karaoke
  • Spanish
  • Cooking
  • Kids TV
  • Volleyball
  • Dance
  • Financial Literacy
  • Karate
  • Soccer
  • Drama
  • Baking
  • Mad Science
  • Yoga
  • Kids Chess
  • Tennis
  • Bricks 4 Kidz
  • Gymnastics
